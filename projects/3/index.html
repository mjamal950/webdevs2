This app was developed to be used as a personal bus tracer for students around the Lane Tech campus. The app allows you to track the arrival times of the 152 Addison going eastbound, 152 Addison going westbound, 49/X49 northbound, and the 49/x49 southbound.

Find My Towed Vehicle

Click here to access
This app was developed to help people find their towed vehicles in the City of Chicago. The app extracts data from the City of Chicago and provides the location of the car. Simply enter the plate numbe of the car and the address and map of the vehicle's location will be provided.

Potholes in the City of Chicago

Click here to access
All throughout the City of Chicago, there are pothole in almost every neighborhood and community area. This app was developed to help people indicate the location and other information of potholes in the city. The app extracts data from the city's data portal and shows the location, creation date, etc. of a particular pothole.

Food Inspections

The City of Chicago is world famous for its renowned restaurant establishments that gives it it's status as a major U.S. tourist destination. However, there are sometimes those select few of establishments that fail to meet certain health standards and requirements. This app extracts data from the data portal and provides information about the various restaurants throughout Chicago, especially the status of their health inspections.
